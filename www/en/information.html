    NuTyX licences

    NuTyX is build around various licences

    The packages of NuTyX

    As NuTyX is a GNU/linux distribution, it contains softwares writen by a big number of developpers around the world. Each package propose its own licence choose by its authors. To know the type of the licence used, please consult the sources code of the concerned package.

    The scripts

    The scripts coming from the projet Linux From Scratch are under the http://lfs.traduc.org/view/lfs...tml>Creative Commons License 2.0. licence. Some scripts are adjusted for the need of NuTyX by Thierry Nuttens.

    The installer (MINI iso)

    The installer is build from scratch by Thierry Nuttens and is under licence http://www.gnu.org/copyleft/gpl.html
